Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the Esma app for?

A: Esma helps you estimate solar panel installation costs, calculate savings, and access solar energy subsidies in Uzbekistan.

Q: Can I calculate how much solar energy I can generate?

A: Yes, Esma includes tools to estimate energy generation based on your roof size, location, and panel type.

Q: How do I know if I qualify for a subsidy?

A: The app provides a calculator that helps determine your eligibility and estimated subsidy amount based on national guidelines.
